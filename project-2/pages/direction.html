Heat oven to 375*. Mix sugar, pumpkin (not pie filling), and shortening. Stir in flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt. Stir in raisins and nuts (optional). Drop dough by teaspoon onto ungreased cookie sheet. Bake until light brown, 8 to 10 minutes. Immediately remove from cookie sheet; cool. 4 dozen.
