Instructions

Diana Creekmore

Mix Together Sugar Pumpkin Shortening
Stir In Flour Baking Powder Baking Soda Cinnamon Salt
Add In Raisins Nuts

The Finish

Heat oven to 375*. Drop dough by teaspoon onto ungreased cookie sheet. Bake until light brown, 8 to 10 minutes. Immediately remove from cookie sheet; cool. 4 dozen.
