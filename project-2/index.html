Baking has always been in my blood. Before I could even read I was making cakes from scratch. I would watch my mom and see what ingredient went in what measuring implement. Simple! I will share with you an easy and delicious cookie recipe.

Pumpkin Raisin Cookies

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup canned pumpkin
  • 1/2 cup shortening
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  • 1/2 cup nuts-optional
    1. walnuts
    2. pecans
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
